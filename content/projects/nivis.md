**Nivis** is a television series that combines live-action footage with 3D content.

A production pipeline was developed covering the stages from **Layout** to **Precompositing**, integrating **Maya**, **Unreal Engine**, and **Arnold**.

A rendering pipeline based on **Unreal Engine RTX** was implemented. Certain render layers were produced in **Arnold** due to **Unreal Engine 4** limitations regarding **AOV** generation.

Additional production tools were developed, including a task management system tailored to the project's requirements, quality control tools, and an application for managing and monitoring render farm jobs.
